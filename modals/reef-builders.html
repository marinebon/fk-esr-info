Percent cover of reef building corals

The status of percent coral cover of reef building species has remained stable over the past five years, though the trends have been in decline overall during the monitoring time period. These reef building species are: Acropora palmata, Acropora cervicornis, Meandrina meandrites, Diploria labrinthyformis, Montastrea cavernosa, Pseudodiploria strigosa, Pseudodiploria clivosa, Colpophylia natans, and Orbicella faveolata for Florida Keys reefs. Source: FWC Fish and Wildlife Research Institute Coral Reef Evaluation and Monitoring Project.
